📖 How to Use

📄

From Document (DOCX/PDF)

  1. ⬆️ Upload your .docx or .pdf file using the upload area on the left sidebar
  2. ✏️ Write a prompt to guide the AI (e.g., "Create slides from this document" or "Summarize into 8 slides")
  3. 🎨 Choose a theme or leave it as "Auto" for AI to pick the best one
  4. ⚡ Click "Generate Slides" and wait for AI to create your presentation
💬

From Prompt (No Document)

  1. ✏️ Write a detailed prompt describing your presentation topic and structure
  2. 💡 Example: "Create 6 slides about Digital Marketing trends 2026 with intro, key trends, strategies, tools, case study, and conclusion"
  3. 🎨 Choose a theme or leave it as "Auto" for AI to pick the best one
  4. ⚡ Click "Generate Slides" — AI will create content from scratch based on your prompt

💡 Tip: After generating, you can edit individual slides, undo changes, and download as PPTX.
