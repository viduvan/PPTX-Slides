A good presentation system helps you do 3 things

It's not that you can't make beautiful slides — it's that you have to redo them every time.

  • ① Unified design language

    All pages share the same tokens — font sizes, colors, shadows stay consistent.

  • ② Lower reuse cost

    Next time on a similar topic, just copy the deck, swap data, change theme.

  • ③ Switchable, presentable

    Press T to cycle themes, A to switch animations — bonus points in meetings.
